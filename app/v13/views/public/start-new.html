Registering an exempt item

Use this service if you have one of the following items to trade:

  • a musical instrument made before 1975 with less than 20% ivory by volume
  • an item made before 1947 with less than 10% ivory by volume
  • a portrait miniature made before 1918 with a surface area smaller than 320 square centimetres
  • an item that a qualifying museum intends to buy or hire

This costs £xx.

You’ll get your registration number as soon as we receive payment. We check registrations retrospectively to ensure they comply with the law.

Applying to certify a rare and important item

You can also use the service if you intend to trade any ivory item made before 1918 that is rare and important.

This costs £xxx and you’ll get an exemption certificate if an expert approves your application, usually within 30 days.

Before you start

You'll need:

  • a photo that shows the whole item
  • extra photos, if it’s not clear from the main photo how much ivory is in the item
  • a debit or credit card to make payment
