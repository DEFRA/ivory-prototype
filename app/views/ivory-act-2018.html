The Ivory Act 2018

It is illegal to sell, hire or buy ivory, unless the item is exempt.

Exemptions to the ivory ban

  • Less than 10% ivory, made before 1947
  • Musical instrument, less than 20% ivory, made before 1975
  • Portrait miniature, made before 1918
  • To be acquired by an accredited museum
  • Rarest and most important of its type
