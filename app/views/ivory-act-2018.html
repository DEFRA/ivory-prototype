The Ivory Act 2018

The UK government has banned the sale of most items containing ivory, to help stop elephant poaching.

However, as long as you buy a permit before each sale, you can still trade in the following:

  • Items with less than 10% ivory made before 1947
  • Musical instruments with less than 20% ivory and made before 1975
  • Portrait miniatures made before 1918
  • Items to be acquired by an accredited museum
  • Items of outstandingly high artistic, cultural or historical value made before 1918
