{{ serviceName }}

Use this service to register or apply for a certificate, so that you can sell or hire elephant ivory items that are not included in the ivory ban.

Published 2 December 2019
From:
Animal and Plant Health Agency
! Warning You can be fined and/or sent to prison if you sell, hire or buy an item containing ivory if it has not been registered or certified.

Check what to do with your ivory item

Before you apply, check what to do with your item.

What you need to apply

You'll need:

  • a photo of the item
  • a debit or credit card

It costs ££ to register and £££ to apply for a certificate.

Start now

Before you start

Read the guide to selling, hiring and buying ivory items

You can register:

  • a musical instrument made before 1975 with leass than 20% ivory
  • an item made before 1947 with less than 10% ivory
  • a portrait miniature made before 1918
  • an item you'll sell or hire to an approved museum

You can apply for a certificate if the item:

  • was made before 1918
  • has outstanding artistic, cultural or historical value

Other ways to apply

You can also register or apply for a certificate using a paper form.
